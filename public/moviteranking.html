The Best Movies According to Zoe

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time.

Where the Crawdads Sing

This movie really spoke to me. The story was incredibly intriguing and kept me glued to the screen.

John Wick

I love the dark humor in these films and the action packed fighting. And you can't forget the music, too!

Pulp Fiction

Great writing, great story, and truly a classic.
